Maya Modelling

Here are some of my leisure time 3d modellings all made in Maya. I'm not a professional modeller, so no giggles please.


  • Tyrannosaurus Rex



    Tyrannosaurus Rex from Jurassic Park modelled in Maya using polygons, then smoothed, then animated (then the animation scrapped because of it looked so funny :)

    This is the T-Rex you are seeing at the top of this site. He is chasing the space shuttle in the logo because he is a space enthusiast.
    A very quick render of the dino with Mental Ray. No texture (color, bump or any kind) is applied here.


  • Space Shuttle



    Space Shuttle Atlantis (or Endeavour?) modelled in Maya using polygons, then smoothed.


    A quick render of the shuttle model with Mental Ray.


  • BMW X6



    BMW X6 modelled in Maya before its auguration, using subdivisions (mostly).


    A quick render of the BMW X6 model with Mental Ray. Yes the render looks bad :)
